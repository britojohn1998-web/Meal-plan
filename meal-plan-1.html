Your 3-Tier Steamer (Middle + Top Tiers Only)

Bottom tier = water only. Middle tier = denser foods needing more steam (rice, soya, chicken). Top tier = lighter foods, veggies, eggs. Cook 2 dishes simultaneously! Induction cooktop: set to 800W-1000W for steaming.

☀️ Week 1 — Morning Shift

Cook at 6 AM before shift & again after 2 PM. No perishables (no paneer/chicken/milk) in morning batch.

Monday
~3000 kcal | 100g protein
🌅 6 AM BREAKFAST (Cook Now)
Steamed Rice + Soya Chunks Curry
~850 kcal 35g protein ⏱ 25 min
Rice 100g (dry) • Soya chunks 60g (dry) • Onion 1 • Tomato 1 • Turmeric, cumin, chilli powder • Oil 1 tsp • Salt
🍳 Step-by-step Instructions
1
Soak soya chunks in hot water for 10 min. Squeeze out water and keep aside.
10 min soak
2
Rinse rice. Add rice + 200ml water in a steel bowl and place on middle tier.
3
Mix soya with chopped onion, tomato, spices, and ½ tsp oil in another bowl. Place on top tier.
4
Fill steamer base with 1 litre water. Set induction to 1000W. Cover and steam.
⏱ 20–22 min
5
Check rice is cooked (grains separate). Mix soya with a spoon. Serve hot.

⚡ Induction + Steamer Timing Guide

🌡️
Setting: Always use 800W–1000W for steaming. 600W will take too long. Start at 1000W until steam builds (3–4 min), then reduce to 800W if water is boiling too vigorously.
💧
Water level: Fill base with 1–1.5 litres. For anything over 25 min cooking, check at 20 min and refill if needed. Running out of water = burning the base.
⏱️
Quick Reference: Eggs = 14 min | Vegetables = 12–18 min | White rice = 20 min | Chicken = 28–30 min | Brown rice = 30 min | Rajma (soaked) = 35 min | Chana (soaked) = 25 min
🔥
Remove lid safely: Always tilt the lid away from you — steam burns are serious. Use a cloth/oven mitt.

🥘 Steamer Pro Tips

🍳
Middle tier = denser, heavier cooking. Rice, soya, chicken, legumes all go here. They need more steam heat.
🥬
Top tier = quick-cook foods. Eggs, spinach, corn, capsicum. These steam faster. Add them partway through if your main dish needs more time.
🥩
Chicken safety: Always steam chicken minimum 28 minutes. Cut the thickest part open — must be completely white, no pink. If unsure, steam 5 more minutes.
🫙
No fridge hack: Soya chunks, peanuts, chickpeas, rajma, rice — all last months without refrigeration. Eggs last 2–3 weeks at room temp. Buy chicken only for the day.

🛒 No-Fridge Strategy

🐔
Chicken: Buy fresh from butcher 2–3 times/week. Cook within 4–5 hours of buying. Never leave raw chicken out more than 2 hours.
🥚
Eggs: Keep in a cool dark spot. Room temperature eggs last 2–3 weeks in India. Don't wash eggs — washing removes protective coating.
🫘
Legumes (chana, rajma): Soak in the morning in a closed container. Cook by evening. Soaked legumes are fine for 8–10 hours at room temp.
🌿
Vegetables: Buy every 2 days. Keep in a jute/cloth bag in a cool corner — they last 2–4 days without fridge in this manner.
