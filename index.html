Your 3-Tier Steamer (Middle + Top Tiers Only)

Bottom tier = water only. Middle tier = denser foods needing more steam (rice, soya, chicken). Top tier = lighter foods, veggies, eggs. Cook 2 dishes simultaneously! Induction cooktop: set to 800W-1000W for steaming.

☀️ Week 1 — Morning Shift

Cook at 6 AM before shift & again after 2 PM. No perishables (no paneer/chicken/milk) in morning batch.

Monday
~3000 kcal | 100g protein
🌅 6 AM BREAKFAST (Cook Now)
Steamed Rice + Soya Chunks Curry
~850 kcal 35g protein ⏱ 25 min
Rice 100g (dry) • Soya chunks 60g (dry) • Onion 1 • Tomato 1 • Turmeric, cumin, chilli powder • Oil 1 tsp • Salt
🍳 Step-by-step Instructions
1
Soak soya chunks in hot water for 10 min. Squeeze out water and keep aside.
10 min soak
2
Rinse rice. Add rice + 200ml water in a steel bowl and place on middle tier.
3
Mix soya with chopped onion, tomato, spices, and ½ tsp oil in another bowl. Place on top tier.
4
Fill steamer base with 1 litre water. Set induction to 1000W. Cover and steam.
⏱ 20–22 min
5
Check rice is cooked (grains separate). Mix soya with a spoon. Serve hot.
🥗 LUNCH (Pack from 6AM cook)
Steamed Sweet Potato + Boiled Chana Salad
~700 kcal 22g protein ⏱ 20 min
Sweet potato 200g • Kabuli chana 80g (soaked overnight) • Lemon juice • Cumin powder • Salt • Coriander leaves
🍳 Instructions (Cook alongside breakfast)
1
Soak chana overnight. Next morning, add soaked chana + 1 cup water in steel bowl → middle tier.
2
Cut sweet potato into chunks → steel bowl → top tier.
⏱ 25 min total steam
3
Once done, mix chana + sweet potato. Add lemon, cumin powder, salt. Pack in tiffin.

Banana + Peanuts

🌙 DINNER (Cook after 2 PM)
Steamed Chicken Breast + Steamed Vegetables + Rice
~1050 kcal 45g protein ⏱ 30 min
Chicken breast ½ whole • Rice 80g (dry) • Carrot 1 • Capsicum ½ • Salt, pepper, chilli flakes • Lemon juice • Garlic 3 cloves
🍳 Step-by-step Instructions
1
Marinate chicken with garlic paste, salt, pepper, chilli flakes, lemon juice. Rest 10 min.
2
Rinse rice. Add rice + 160ml water in steel bowl → middle tier.
3
Place marinated chicken on middle tier plate (next to rice or on a separate small plate if it fits).
4
Chop carrot + capsicum. Place vegetables in steel bowl → top tier.
5
Fill base with 1.5L water. Induction 1000W. Steam 28–30 min.
⏱ 28–30 min — chicken must be fully white inside, no pink
6
Drizzle ½ tsp olive oil or coconut oil over vegetables. Serve with rice.
Tuesday
~3000 kcal | 100g protein
🌅 6 AM BREAKFAST
Steamed Eggs (4) + Ragi Roti (no fry — steam-heat)
~700 kcal 28g protein ⏱ 15 min
Eggs 4 • Ragi flour 80g • Salt • Cumin • Spinach handful (add to ragi dough)
🍳 Instructions
1
Place 4 whole eggs (in shell) directly on top tier steamer rack.
2
Make ragi dough: ragi flour + salt + cumin + water (no yeast). Make flat discs, wrap in banana leaf or foil, place on middle tier.
3
Induction 1000W. Steam 12–14 min. Eggs will be hard-boiled. Roti will be steamed soft.
⏱ 14 min
4
Peel eggs. Sprinkle with pepper + salt. Eat with ragi roti.
